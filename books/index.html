Ice Tomb

  • More About Ice Tomb

 

 

Deep within the Antarctic Ice Sheet, a hotspot suddenly develops on satellite tracking and the science team sent to investigate disappears. Erica Daniels, an esteemed volcanologist, must puzzle out the origin of this unusual heat signature alongside a vast graveyard of frozen bodies before she is numbered among them. She enlists a clever team of scientists, but ultimately will need to call upon the man she despises—NASA’s David Marsh.

Sinkhole

  • More About Sinkhole

A team of scientists and cave explorers, while investigating one of the deepest caves in the world, become trapped and must rely on a phobic doctor and a Mayan revolutionary to come to their rescue. In their descent, the rescuers encounter sewer pipe crawlways, chambers drenched in snottites—acidic bacterial clusters that develop in sulfur-rich caves—and toxic gas, thirty-six-story pitches, fast-flowing sumps and crystalline cathedrals… and a mystery that may leave them buried 2000 meters beneath the surface of the earth.

Time Meddlers

  • More About Time Meddlers

Time Meddlers is a science fiction series. The first novel begins with two children, Sarah Sachs and Matt Barnes, who, while searching for Matt's missing father, find the scientist's time machine. They become trapped in the machine by a diabolical villain and must fend for their lives in the forest-matted land of North America during the 1600s. Their adventure takes them face to face with warring Indians - the Algonquin tribe and the Mohawk - and the explorer, Champlain.

Time Meddlers Undercover

  • More About Time Meddlers Undercover

One of Canada’s top scientists has found the secret to time travel. But something has gone horribly wrong. . . . again.

While breaking into his old house, thirteen-year-old Matt Barnes finds a microfilm that connects his family to a spy in World War II. Then his time-traveling father is yanked to the same time period, in occupied Amsterdam. The only way Matt can rescue his father is to travel to this time, and the only person who can help him is his best friend, Sarah Sachs. When Matt and Sarah land in the war-torn country, they’re immediately caught up in a world of high intrigue and dangerous covert operations.

Time Meddlers on the Nile

  • More About Time Meddlers on the Nile

Thirteen-year-old Matt Barnes and Sarah Sachs, while attempting to rescue Matt's father from multiple universes, face an even more challenging obstacle: the erasure of their own timeline. What can they do before they, themselves, are erased?

Somewhere along the Nile the two teens must prevent the ultimate meddling during the time of Nubian pharaohs and princes, palaces and temples to rival the ancient Egyptians and fearsome desert raiders. They must puzzle out the moment and event where Matt's father or Nadine, his archnemesis, interfered with history. If they don't, it could mean the end of history as we know it, or a never-ending loop of time.

Warning: Read this book only if you have a strong stomach, as it is sprinkled with the occasional mummified remains.

Mosaic

  • More About Mosaic

A car accident leaves Erin Rocheford, a seventeen-year-old hockey player, fractured, disfigured, near death.

Erin's parents decide that a vacation in Florida, on an island of palm trees and pirate lore, is just what she needs to recover. But in her post-traumatic state, Erin is vulnerable to attack, to a ghostly invasion, and to a further fragmenting of her troubled grey matter. Soon after arriving on the island, a tale of an odd English pirate and his feisty captive—a story of defiance and decapitation—weaves itself into her mind, threatening her very soul.
