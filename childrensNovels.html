Time Meddlers

  • More About Time Meddlers

Time Meddlers is a science fiction series. The first novel begins with two children, Sarah Sachs and Matt Barnes, who, while searching for Matt's missing father, find the scientist's time machine. They become trapped in the machine by a diabolical villain and must fend for their lives in the forest-matted land of North America during the 1600s. Their adventure takes them face to face with warring Indians - the Algonquin tribe and the Mohawk - and the explorer, Champlain.

Time Meddlers Undercover

  • More About Time Meddlers Undercover

One of Canada’s top scientists has found the secret to time travel. But something has gone horribly wrong. . . . again.

While breaking into his old house, thirteen-year-old Matt Barnes finds a microfilm that connects his family to a spy in World War II. Then his time-traveling father is yanked to the same time period, in occupied Amsterdam. The only way Matt can rescue his father is to travel to this time, and the only person who can help him is his best friend, Sarah Sachs. When Matt and Sarah land in the war-torn country, they’re immediately caught up in a world of high intrigue and dangerous covert operations.

Ghost in the Piano

  • Read Chapter One

A troubled girl, a zany medium, a determined reporter and a villainous ghost - these are the main characters in a very bizarre ghost story. Ghost in the Piano is a mystery that will take the reader along with twelve-year-old Jenna from the sleepy little town of Prudence to the ghost-ridden halls of the Tower of London.

Ghost in the Piano is a work in progress
