Fees

 


I charge $25/hour. I don't charge per word or per page, because you may have many pages that require absolutely no work.


Since I can't determine how long it will take me to edit your manuscript, I do require a deposit for a book-length manuscript of $100 prior to commencing work, another deposit halfway through, and the remainder when I've completed the edit. However, I will edit the first chapter of a book-length manuscript before we begin to show you the quality of my services. For a short story, or something of a technical nature, I will do the work initially, return a few pages and then require payment before returning the remainder.


Everything will be done online, manuscripts sent through email. Sorry, I don't take print copies through the regular postal service since I can mark your manuscript much easier in Word, rather than navigate through formatting issues.

Email me first, and we can discuss the arrangements. Payments with credit card can be made through PayPal via an invoice to your email account.
