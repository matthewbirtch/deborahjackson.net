Ice Tomb

  • More About Ice Tomb

The year is 2015. Deep within the Antarctic Ice Sheet a hotspot suddenly appears on satellite tracking. The US science team that is sent from McMurdo Station to investigate finds an icy graveyard. Minutes later, their transmission is cut off. The last sounds heard over the radio are their screams.

NASA lures volcanologist Erica Daniels to a conference in Houston by promising to consider her for their upcoming mission - establishing the first moon base. Instead, her archrival and ex-lover, David Marsh, gets the plum assignment, while she's sent to Antarctica to lead a new team beneath the ice. Even worse, she's sent in blind.

They told her about the thermal signature, but not about the bodies. Where did they come from? They don't belong to the missing investigative team, so what has become of her fellow scientists? Is the activity under the ice the remnants of an ancient civilization or is there a more sinister explanation? The answer could mean the very survival of the human race. To find out Erica will have to join forces with the man she despises--a man who's on the moon.

Sinkhole

  • Read Chapter One

A team of scientists and cave explorers, while investigating one of the deepest caves in the world, become trapped and must rely on a phobic doctor and a Mayan revolutionary to come to their rescue. In their descent, the rescuers encounter sewer pipe crawlways, chambers drenched in snottites—acidic bacterial clusters that develop in sulfur-rich caves—and toxic gas, thirty-six-story pitches, fast-flowing sumps and crystalline cathedrals… and a mystery that may leave them buried 2000 meters beneath the surface of the earth.

Sinkhole is a work in progress

Time Meddlers

  • More About Time Meddlers

Time Meddlers is a science fiction series. The first novel begins with two children, Sarah Sachs and Matt Barnes, who, while searching for Matt's missing father, find the scientist's time machine. They become trapped in the machine by a diabolical villain and must fend for their lives in the forest-matted land of North America during the 1600s. Their adventure takes them face to face with warring Indians - the Algonquin tribe and the Mohawk - and the explorer, Champlain.

Time Meddlers Undercover

  • More About Time Meddlers Undercover

One of Canada’s top scientists has found the secret to time travel. But something has gone horribly wrong. . . . again.

While breaking into his old house, thirteen-year-old Matt Barnes finds a microfilm that connects his family to a spy in World War II. Then his time-traveling father is yanked to the same time period, in occupied Amsterdam. The only way Matt can rescue his father is to travel to this time, and the only person who can help him is his best friend, Sarah Sachs. When Matt and Sarah land in the war-torn country, they’re immediately caught up in a world of high intrigue and dangerous covert operations.

Ghost in the Piano

  • Read Chapter One

A troubled girl, a zany medium, a determined reporter and a villainous ghost - these are the main characters in a very bizarre ghost story. Ghost in the Piano is a mystery that will take the reader along with twelve-year-old Jenna from the sleepy little town of Prudence to the ghost-ridden halls of the Tower of London.

Ghost in the Piano is a work in progress
